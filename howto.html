User Manual - Taskify

Adding To-Do Items:

To add a new to-do item, simply type the task in the input field and press "Enter".

Marking Tasks as Done:

To mark a task as complete, click on the task itself. This action will add a linethrough in the task, indicating it's done.

Removing Tasks:

To remove a task, click on the 'x' icon beside the task. This action will instantly remove the task from your list.

Stay Organized:

Keep your tasks organized by utilizing these features to manage your to-do list efficiently.
